<h2>Why Choose LED Wall Lights?</h2>
<p>Switching to LED technology can reduce household energy consumption significantly. Here is why they are perfect for Indian homes:</p>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Cost-Effective:</strong> Massive savings on electricity bills.</li>
    <li><strong>Longevity:</strong> Lasts 20,000 to 50,000 hours.</li>
    <li><strong>Low Heat Emission:</strong> Perfect for the Indian climate.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Buying Guide: What to Look For</h2>
<h3>1. IP Rating (Ingress Protection)</h3>
<p>If you are buying for balconies, look for an <strong>IP65 rating</strong>. This ensures the light is dust-tight and protected against water, crucial for the Indian monsoon.</p>

<h3>2. Color Temperature (CCT)</h3>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Warm White (2700K - 3000K):</strong> Best for bedrooms/living rooms.</li>
    <li><strong>Cool White (5000K - 6500K):</strong> Ideal for study rooms/kitchens.</li>
</ul>
